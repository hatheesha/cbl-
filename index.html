BISCUITS COMPANY

Ceylon Biscuits Limited

CBL is a Sri Lankan food manufacturing conglomerate that produces high-quality confectionery and convenient nutrition. Our story started in 1968 with the protein-enriched Care biscuit created in collaboration between CBL, the Sri Lankan Government, and CARE U.S.A., to combat malnutrition in schoolchildren at the time. Since then, we have worked closely with communities across the country to understand food needs and apply our core values to making food that truly enriches lives.

History

Simon (Arthur) Wickramasingha (1902-1984), a planter, took over a small biscuit factory in the 1930s from a local businessman named Williams. This handmade biscuit operation began at a time when all biscuits in the local market were imported.

We are committed to making food we are proud of. We want our food to help build better nutrition habits in our communities, and we’re innovating new ways to create great food that our consumers can trust. Using the best ingredients, including more locally grown fresh produce, we’re setting new standards every day in safety, quality, and taste, helping people make informed, good food choices at every meal of the day.
